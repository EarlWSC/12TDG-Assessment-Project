WRX
World RallyX Championship

Watch Live Youtube

The World RallyX Championship is a Short form, high intensity racing series held on mixed surface circuits (tarmac, gravel or dirt.) It features head-to-head races with standing starts, jumps and extreme and legal contact. Combining rally car tech with side by side circuit racing.
Since 2022, WRX has turned fully electric, named the RX1e class. These electric beasts produce 680 horsepower and an instant 880Nm of torque. But back then before the electric switch, the internal combustion engines of the RX1’s and Supercars were as powerful and better sounding than what we have now.

    As the lights turn green, the rallycross race begins. The Black Monster and Italian RX1e's have a good start off the line.   • Marvelous Last to First Overtake with a calculated pull

    Kevin Eriksson found himself to be 4th on the 6 lap race. Though a short race, Kevin Eriksson proved that it only takes 1 corner to take back his rightful first place position.       • Notable Drivers

      Johan Kristoffersson – 7× World RX Champion (2017–2018, 2020–2024); all-time leader in event wins (47) and podiums (64).

      Timmy Hansen – 2019 World RX Champion; over 49 podium finishes and 14 event wins.

      Mattias Ekström – 2016 World RX Champion; 12 event wins.

      Petter Solberg – 2× World RX Champion (2014–2015); 10 event wins.

      Sébastien Loeb – Multiple event wins and podiums (17 podiums).

      Niclas Grönholm – 9 event wins; active and among current top contenders.


      Notable Manufacturers & Chassis


      Volkswagen – Top manufacturer in World RX history, with 50 event wins (mainly via Polo model).

      Peugeot – Second-most event wins (21), strong presence with 208 model.

      Audi – Significant winner tally (17 wins), especially with S1.

      Citroën – Earned 9 event wins (notably with DS3).

      Hyundai – Gained 7 wins with the i20 RX.

      Ford – Moderate success with 6 wins via Fiesta platforms.

      QEV Technologies – Emerging with 4 wins as a chassis/provider.

      PWR Racing – Has secured 3 event wins as a team/manufacturer.

  • Höljes Round 2 FINAL 2020

    One of the closest RallyX finals in RallyX History, who comes out on top?

    The 2nd to Last Lap of the Final Race of RallyX 2020, Kristoffersson had set the final Joker Lap of the race and was expected to come out behind of Ekstrom. And it was so but the pressure was on Ekstrom as he had to defend against Kristoffersson for the final lap. As Kristoffersson closed in and sniffed for the overtake, the checkered flag was seen waving and the weight on Ekstrom's back was lifted as he takes the win at Holjes. Kristoffersson was close, only 0.197s behind Ekstrom.
