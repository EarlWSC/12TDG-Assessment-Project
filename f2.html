Formula 2

Watch Live Youtube

Formula 2 is the official feeder series to Formula 1, designed to prepare young drivers for the pinnacle of motorsport. It’s a spec series, meaning all teams use the same chassis (Dallara), engine (Mecachrome 3.4L V6), and tires (Pirelli), ensuring that driver skill is the defining factor. F2 cars are open-wheel single-seaters with a carbon fiber monocoque chassis and a naturally aspirated V6 engine producing around 620 horsepower. They reach speeds of up to 335 km/h, and while not as fast as F1, they offer intense wheel-to-wheel racing and strategic depth. The series runs alongside Formula 1 at select Grand Prix weekends, giving drivers exposure to F1 teams and fans. Many current F1 stars—like Charles Leclerc, George Russell, and Oscar Piastri—graduated from F2.

Spa 2024 Sprint Race

  • Immense Bravery from Antonelli

    In yet another rain-soaked race, this time at Spa, Andrea Kimi Antonelli goes on a head-on-head fight between Franco Colapinto up through Eau Rouge in the rain. His impressive determination to get the move done as they go side by side at the most dangerous high-speed corner in the wet deserves recognition.

    • Baku 2022 Sprint Race

  • Chaos in the Castle Section

    The narrow streets of Baku always promise drama, and in 2022, a multi-car pileup in the castle section saw four drivers retire within seconds. Amid the chaos, Frederik Vesti navigated through the carnage and took his maiden F2 win.

  • Monza 2023 Feature Race

    Bearman’s Last-Lap Divebomb for the Win

    In the closing laps of the Monza Feature Race, PREMA Racing’s Oliver Bearman was trailing Enzo Fittipaldi. On the final lap, Bearman executed a daring divebomb into Turn 1, locking up slightly but managing to hold the line and snatch victory. The move was hailed as one of the boldest overtakes of the season..

  • Silverstone 2024 Feature Race

    Antonelli’s Wet Weather Masterclass

    In a rain-soaked Silverstone, Mercedes junior Andrea Kimi Antonelli showcased supreme car control, overtaking three cars in one lap and managing tire degradation perfectly to take a commanding win.
