NASCAR

Watch Live Youtube

The National Association for Stock Car Auto Racing (NASCAR) is America’s popular motorsport. The top tier of NASCAR, the Cup Series, features 36 races per season across a large variety and difference of tracks like oval courses, short tracks, intermediates and recently, street and road courses! NASCAR features a 670 Horsepower V8 N/A Engine with a 5-speed sequential gearbox. With a stock chassis resembling American manufactured road cars, they were made with abilities to pack tightly, bump draft and be aggressive on track.

      • Notable Drivers

      Richard Petty – 7× NASCAR Cup Series Champion (1964, 1967, 1971, 1972, 1974, 1975, 1979); all-time leader with 200 Cup wins.

      Dale Earnhardt – 7× Champion (1980, 1986, 1987, 1990, 1991, 1993, 1994); one of NASCAR’s most iconic and influential drivers.

      Jimmie Johnson – 7× Champion (2006–2010, 2013, 2016); only driver to win five consecutive titles.

      Jeff Gordon – 4× Champion (1995, 1997, 1998, 2001); helped usher NASCAR into a modern era of popularity.

      Joey Logano – 3× Champion (2018, 2022, 2024); reigning Cup Champion and active top contender.

      Cale Yarborough – 3× Champion (1976–1978); first driver to win three straight titles.


      Notable Manufacturers


      Chevrolet – Most manufacturer championships and wins in NASCAR.

      Ford – A strong and historic presence across NASCAR with multiple titles.

      Toyota – Emerging powerhouse in recent years, especially in Truck and Xfinity series.

      Buick, Dodge, Pontiac – Contributed to NASCAR’s championship history in earlier decades.
