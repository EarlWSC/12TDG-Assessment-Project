World Endurance
Championship

Watch Live Youtube

The World Endurance Championship (WEC) is all about endurance over a long period of time. The wits of the driver, skills of the engineers and the reliability of the machine will decide their fate. These races run from a range of 6 - 24 hours. Iconic races like the 6 Hours of Spa & 24 Hours of Le Mans make up the core of WEC. WEC runs multiple races during a single race with each ‘class’ competing for their own title.
There are typically 3 classes than partake the long awaited race:
Hypercar - Top tier prototypes, the fastest in WEC.
LMP2 - Spec-series prototypes; everyone has the same car with driver skill to account for winning.
LMGT3 - GT class of sports cars (e.g. Ferrari 296, Aston Martin Vantage, Mclaren 720s)

6H of Spa-Francorchamps 2025

  • 3-way battle for P2 between the Hypercars.

    Nearing the final hours of the 6 hours of Spa, The Alpine caught up to the 2nd place Ferrari, with the BMW close behind them. This opportunity had made a 3-way fight for 2nd place a reality. Weaving through traffic and going door to door at some of the toughest corners in racing, the Ferrari defends his position with the #36 Alpine in 3rd place; a podium position to be proud of!

    • 8H of Bahrain 2024

  • A full lap fight between the top contenders in LMGT3

    In the final hour of the race, the Ferrari’s and Corvette dominated the lead. Fighting for 1st, both the main cars of Ferrari and Corvette fought from the beginning of the pit straight. For half the lap, they were door to door, switching positions every corner but in the end, the corvette had the pace advantage, taking the lead and eventually winning the race.
