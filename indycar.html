IndyCar

Watch Live Youtube

Indycar is the highest level of open-wheel racing in America. Officially known as the NTT INDYCAR Series, it offers high-speed oval racing with races like the legendary Indianapolis 500, technical tracks like Road America and tight street circuits. IndyCar features a 2.2L twin-turbocharged V6 Engine manufactured by Honda and Chevrolet. It produces ~700-750 Horsepower with a push-to-pass system that DOESN’T use a battery, but rather uses the maximum power output of the engine. Unlike in Formula 1 where teams manufacture their own car, IndyCar is a Spec-Series; meaning everyone uses the same chassis with a choice of 2 different engine suppliers: Honda and Chevrolet.

    Massive Pileup, Kyle Kirkwood found rolling upside down.

    On Lap 183, a huge crash took out multiple cars on the front row. One of them was Kyle Kirkwood, who violently flipped and slid upside down into the barriers, narrowly missing fans as a loose tire was sent straight up, flying. With all safety devices, Kyle walked off unharmed.
