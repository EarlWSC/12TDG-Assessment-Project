GT World
Challenge

Watch Live Youtube

The GT World Challenge is a global series of the most advanced GT racing, sanctioned by SRO Motorsports. It features GT3 (like LMGT3 & GTD/Pro from WEC & IMSA) & GT4 Class racecars. They are basically favourable legal-sports cars turned into racing machines!
The series itself is split into 4 regions across the world:
GTWC Europe
GTWC Asia
GTWC America
GTWC Australia.
With each having their own calendar at challenging circuits like The Nordschliefe, Spa-Francorchamps, Mount Paronama having tight regional championship fights, you can expect to see one of the best GT battles and overtakes in motorsport!

  • GTWC Europe - Monza 2025

    Traffic Paradise at the Monza Pit-straight

    With so many young talents and old golds joining the European Racing scene, this sight was found in the first seconds of GTWC Monza 2025. With a total of more than 50 cars, traffic paradise would soon start to show into the first corner of Monza - Notorious for its uncertain safety for many drivers, especially in the middle of the traffic. As the cars took the first chicane, many cars chose to take the escape route and others driving onto the grass to avoid any unnecessary collision. With so much traffic, you couldn't even rejoin back onto the track with a car right next to you at any given moment!

  • GTWC America - COTA 2025

    Looking the wrong way mate!

    As the green flag waved, the race begun at Circuit of the Americas. Headed into the first turn, the #91 Mercedes-AMG GT3 was spun around in the first corner. With the race being only an hour, the driver of the #91 Mercedes-AMG GT3 had no hopes of retaking his position and possibilities of winning. The driver who spun them out was handed a 10 second time penalty.

  • GTWC Asia - Sepang 2025

    Parked at a.. Racing Circuit?

    Another Turn 1 incident, this time at the Asian GTWC Circuit of Sepang in Malaysia. This incident at Sepang involved 2 Porsche 992 GT3's and 1 Audi R8 GT3. They were pushed off and were stranded on the ungrippable and wet grass, forcing the race director to call a full course yellow to neutralize the race and clear off the 3 GT3's who were stranded.
