GT World
Challenge

Watch Live Youtube

The GT World Challenge is a global series of the most advanced GT racing, sanctioned by SRO Motorsports. It features GT3 (like LMGT3 & GTD/Pro from WEC & IMSA) & GT4 Class racecars. They are basically favourable legal-sports cars turned into racing machines!
The series itself is split into 4 regions across the world:
GTWC Europe
GTWC Asia
GTWC America
GTWC Australia.
With each having their own calendar at challenging circuits like The Nordschliefe, Spa-Francorchamps, Mount Paronama having tight regional championship fights, you can expect to see one of the best GT battles and overtakes in motorsport!
